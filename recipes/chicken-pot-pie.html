Chicken Pot Pie

Description

Chicken pot pie is the ultimate comfort food, offering the perfect combination of creamy, savory filling and a flaky, buttery crust. It’s the kind of dish that warms you up from the inside out, with tender chicken, vegetables, and a rich, flavorful sauce all baked into one cozy package. Whether you're making it for a family dinner or a special occasion, chicken pot pie is sure to impress.

It's a hearty, satisfying meal that brings together simple ingredients in a way that feels indulgent yet homey. The best part? It can be customized with your favorite veggies or seasonings, making it as comforting and flavorful as you want.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked, diced chicken breast
  • 1 (15 ounce) can mixed vegetables, drained
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 (9-inch) deep-dish frozen pie crusts, thawed

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Combine chicken, mixed vegetables, condensed soup, and milk in a bowl.
  4. Pour the mixture into the bottom pie crust.
  5. Cover with second crust. Seal the edges and poke holes in top crust.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown, about 30 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
