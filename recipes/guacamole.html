Guacamole

Description

In no time, you can make a delicious, fresh guacamole that's perfect for any occasion. Quick, simple, and full of fresh taste, it's the perfect go-to snack or appetizer when you're short on time but still craving something tasty!

Guacamole is one of those perfect foods that's both simple and satisfying. What makes guacamole stand out is how easily it can be tailored to your taste, with the balance of creamy, tangy, and spicy elements that come together in every bite. Plus, it's packed with fresh ingredients that offer a healthy boost, making it not only delicious but a great addition to any meal or snack. Whether for a quick snack or a crowd-pleasing appetizer, guacamole is always a good idea.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  3. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  4. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
